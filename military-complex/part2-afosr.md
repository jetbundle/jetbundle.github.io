# Part 2: Air Force Office of Scientific Research (AFOSR)

## Establishment and Mission

AFOSR was established in 1951 to fund basic research relevant to Air Force needs, particularly in mathematics, physics, and engineering.

## Key Contributions

### Control Theory

- Funded research in optimal control and dynamic programming
- Supported Kalman filtering and state estimation

### Optimization

- Funded linear programming and operations research
- Supported development of optimization algorithms

## Impact

AFOSR's support was crucial for the development of modern control theory and optimization methods.
