# Executive Summary

This report provides archival verification of the relationship between U.S. defense agencies and academic research in applied mathematics and computer science during the post-WWII era.

## Key Findings

- **Office of Naval Research (ONR)**: Established 1946, funded early numerical analysis and computing
- **Air Force Office of Scientific Research (AFOSR)**: Established 1951, funded control theory and optimization
- **Advanced Research Projects Agency (ARPA)**: Established 1958, funded interactive computing and networking

## Methodology

Archival research from:
- Defense agency annual reports
- University research contracts
- Historical publications
- Declassified documents
