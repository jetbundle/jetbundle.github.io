# Part 1: Office of Naval Research (ONR)

## Establishment and Mission

The Office of Naval Research was established in 1946 as the first U.S. agency dedicated to funding basic scientific research in universities.

## Key Contributions

### Numerical Analysis

- Funded early computational methods for solving differential equations
- Supported development of numerical linear algebra techniques

### Computing Infrastructure

- Supported ENIAC and early scientific computing projects
- Funded development of numerical libraries and algorithms

## Impact

ONR's funding model established the pattern for defense-academic collaboration that continues today.
