# Part 3: Advanced Research Projects Agency (ARPA)

## Establishment and Mission

ARPA (later DARPA) was established in 1958 in response to Sputnik, with a mandate to fund high-risk, high-reward research.

## Key Contributions

### Interactive Computing

- Funded Project MAC at MIT
- Supported development of time-sharing systems
- Enabled interactive computing paradigm

### Networking

- Funded ARPANET development
- Supported packet-switching research
- Enabled distributed computing

## Impact

ARPA's Information Processing Techniques Office (IPTO) fundamentally shaped modern computing.
