### Key Sources

- **ONR Math Branch**: Established 1946, funded foundational computing and mathematics research
- **DARPA IPTO**: Information Processing Techniques Office, founded 1963 by J.C.R. Licklider
- **AFOSR**: Air Force Office of Scientific Research, established 1949
- **Project SCOOP**: Scientific Computation of Optimum Programs, U.S. Air Force, 1947

### Methodology

This cartography is compiled from:
