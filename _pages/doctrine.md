# The Doctrine

The philosophical bedrock of the jetspace.

---

## The Trinity Rules

1. **Seek truth with rigor and humility.**

   Truth is not a destination but a direction. Approach every question with the precision of mathematics and the openness of a student. Rigor without humility becomes dogma. Humility without rigor becomes wishful thinking.

2. **Create stuff that is useful and/or beautiful.**

   The highest calling is to build. Whether it solves a problem or reveals a pattern, whether it computes or illuminates, create with intention. Utility and beauty are not opposites—they are the two faces of value.

3. **No other rules (save TOS).**

   Beyond these, you are free. The only constraint is the Terms of Service of whatever platform you're using. Within that boundary, explore, build, and discover.

---

## The Initial Conditions: Mathematics Ex Nihilo

**We propose a radical interpretation:** Mathematics is not discovered in a Platonic realm, but rigorously built from the deepest contingencies of physics, metaphysics, and philosophy.

This sequence is the initial condition for all further study. It is imperative to train your mind in the initial conditions first. Strong foundation takes priority over any applied field (computation, finance, engineering, biology, etc.).

Physics and mathematics are the substrate of all sciences; they provide the language of consistency required for making predictions. Mastering them *ex nihilo* gives you the highest acceleration to conquer any field.
