# Join the Bundle

We are building a community of high-knowledge, high-skilled individuals who collaborate, research, and innovate through mastery of **Physics, Finance, and Computation**.

## Mission

The goal of `jetbundle` is to attract and cultivate a community that:

- **Masters the fundamentals**: Physics, Finance, and Computation as the core disciplines for 21st-century scientists and engineers
- **Builds freely**: Creates open-source tools, simulations, and research
- **Thinks independently**: Free thinking, free building, engineering excellence
- **Flourishes**: Eudaimonia through knowledge, collaboration, and innovation

---

## How to Contribute

### 1. Join the Discussion

- **GitHub Discussions**: [Join the conversation](https://github.com/jetbundle/jetbundle.github.io/discussions)
- Share insights, ask questions, collaborate on projects

### 2. Contribute to the Archive

- Submit Pull Requests to add declassified memos
- Add booklist entries and primary sources
- Help expand the historical record

### 3. Contribute to the Toolkit

- Fork the [simulations repository](https://github.com/jetbundle/simulations)
- Build new simulations and tools
- Share your implementations of the "lemmas"

### 4. Write for the Blog

- Share research findings
- Document project progress
- Write technical deep dives

## Community Guidelines

### Code of Conduct

- **Respect**: Treat all community members with respect
- **Openness**: Welcome diverse perspectives and backgrounds
- **Excellence**: Strive for high-quality contributions
- **Collaboration**: Work together to build something greater

### Contribution Guidelines

1. **Fork and Clone**: Fork the repository and clone locally
2. **Create Branch**: Create a feature branch for your work
3. **Make Changes**: Implement your changes with clear commits
4. **Submit PR**: Open a Pull Request with a clear description
5. **Engage**: Respond to feedback and iterate
