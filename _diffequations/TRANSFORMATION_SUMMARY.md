### 5. Math Formatting Updates

All mathematical uses of `|` replaced with `\mid`:
- `$\left|f(x,y_{1}) - f(x,y_{2})\right|$` → `$\mid f(x,y_{1}) - f(x,y_{2}) \mid$`
- `$\left|y\right|$` → `$\mid y \mid$`
- `$\ln \left|y\right|$` → `$\ln \mid y \mid$`
- `$\left|\frac{y}{K - y}\right|$` → `$\mid \frac{y}{K - y} \mid$`
