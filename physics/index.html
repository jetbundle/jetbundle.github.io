physics

this is an inner exploration of my working mental image of physics.

it makes the most sense to start with things that are considered to be basic. you will waste a lot of time if you allow yourself to move on before mastery of the basics. the meticulous, detailed completion and constant mental revision of Landau 1 and 2 should really change how you motivate ontological directions in mathematics entirely. the meaning of least action, geometric symmetries, conservation laws, and relativistic invariance are enforced by a structured, epistemic approach to particles/fields and their backgrounds.
