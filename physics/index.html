physics

this is a section of my internal adventure to build a working mental image of physics in all of her beauty. this page will frequently be updated, and changed overtime

it makes the most sense to start with things that are considered to be basic to the mathematical and physical phenomena detailed by the 20th century. you will waste a lot of time and be a circularly reasoning cringe beurocrat physicist/mathematician if you allow yourself to move on before mastery of the basics, atleast from a conceptual vantage point. don't be like me and retrospectively fill the gaps that could have been done earlier lol. i've suffered enough. the meticulous, detailed completion and constant mental revision of landau vol. 1 and 2 should really change how you motivate the ontological and philosphical directions of mathematics. the trancendental presuppositions of least action, geometric symmetry, conservation, and relativistic invariance are enforced by a structured, terse approach to particles/fields and their curved background geometry first and foremost. a principled development of einsteinian relativity in both the physical and mathematical sense is the only correct approach to ALL modern geometric theories, namely: Kaluza-Klein, Yang Mills, Spin Geometry, and GUTs.

once you have walked the classical arc in basics—variation through global forms—the next layer is to name the mathematical machinery that was silently doing the work all along. mathematical physics is a structural companion: same voice, but the camera turns toward categories, multilinear algebra, manifolds, cohomology, and the symplectic/riemannian backbones that make the physics legible. physics still asks the questions; math is the terse language forced by those constraints.
