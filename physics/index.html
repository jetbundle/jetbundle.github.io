whether you walked the straight path or blindly leapt into axioms the way i once leapt into a divine first love, the revenge arc of physics now lives in classical physics. it is the same story of L&L vol. 1 and 2 (iykyk) and general path of the anisomorphic portal readings... only i attempt to create path-dependent, dissipative cosmic roadtrip through: symmetry and action principles, tensors and important zoo of multilinear pairings, manifolds when coordinates begin to lie you, cohomology when topology holds a grudge and obstructs your mental health, symplectic drama on the cotangent bundle, minkowski and maxwell while spacetime is still a flat background, then curvature when gravity quits pretending to be a background force. i try to be impressionistic where the imagination can help you navigate the profound physics, and strive to be precise where the definitions to defend against belligerents, duelers, and adversarial cringe people who call themselves physicists/mathematicians.
