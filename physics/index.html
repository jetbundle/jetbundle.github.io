whether you walked the straight path or blindly leapt into axioms the way i once leapt into a first love who answered in riddles, the consolidated classical arc now lives in classical physics. it is the same story the older narrative and structural drafts were telling, only with the jokes turned up and the logical spine forced into one road trip: symmetry and action, tensors and pairings, manifolds when coordinates lie, cohomology when topology holds a grudge, symplectic drama on the cotangent bundle, minkowski and maxwell while spacetime is still flat, then curvature when gravity stops pretending to be a background force. impressionistic where the picture helps, precise where the definition bites.
